Medical Defense

Pharmacological strategies and the power of monitoring.

First-Line Therapies

ACE Inhibitors / ARBs

Relax blood vessels by blocking angiotensin formation or action.

-10-15 mmHg

Calcium Channel Blockers

Prevent calcium from entering heart and vessel walls, relaxing them.

-8-12 mmHg

Thiazide Diuretics

Help kidneys remove excess fluid and sodium from the body.

-12-15 mmHg

Beta-Blockers

Reduce heart rate and the heart's workload (often 2nd line).

-10-12 mmHg

Monitoring Protocol

  • Home Monitoring: More accurate than office readings; avoids "White Coat" effect.
  • Consistency: Measure at same time daily.
  • Adherence: Improves medication compliance by 30-40%.
Target Goal: < 130/80 mmHg
