The Cost of Silence

Uncontrolled hypertension damages every major organ system.

Cardiovascular

Heart failure (75% risk) and Myocardial Infarction.
Arteries harden and narrow (Atherosclerosis).

Cerebrovascular

Ischemic and Hemorrhagic Stroke.
60-80% of strokes are hypertension-related.

Renal (Kidneys)

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD).
Hypertension causes 35% of all kidney failure.

Retinal (Eyes)

Hypertensive Retinopathy.
Damage to blood vessels in the retina leads to vision loss.
