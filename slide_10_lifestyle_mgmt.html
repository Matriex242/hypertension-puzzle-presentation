Reclaiming Control

Evidence-based lifestyle interventions to lower pressure.

DASH Diet & Sodium

Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension. Rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and low-fat dairy.

Limit Sodium < 1,500 mg/day
-8-14 mmHg Reduction

Physical Activity

Regular aerobic exercise strengthens the heart, allowing it to pump blood with less effort.

150 min/week Moderate Intensity
-5-8 mmHg Reduction

Weight Management

Blood pressure rises as body weight increases. Losing even a small amount of weight can make a huge difference.

-1 mmHg per 1 kg lost
-5-20 mmHg (per 10kg)

Moderation & Stress

Limiting alcohol intake and managing chronic stress through mindfulness or therapy.

Limit Alcohol: 2 (M) / 1 (W) drinks
-2-4 mmHg Reduction
