Chronic Kidney Disease

Kidneys lose the ability to regulate sodium and fluid balance.

90% of CKD patients develop hypertension.

Thyroid Disorders

Hyperthyroidism increases cardiac output and metabolic rate, driving up pressure.

Direct hormonal impact on heart rate.

Endocrine Disorders

Conditions like Hyperaldosteronism cause salt retention.

Account for 5-10% of secondary hypertension.

Obstructive Sleep Apnea

Repeated oxygen drops at night trigger "fight or flight" responses.

Activates sympathetic nervous system 30-50x/night.

Metabolic Syndrome

Insulin resistance leads to sodium retention and vascular stiffness.

Affects 25-30% of the global population.
